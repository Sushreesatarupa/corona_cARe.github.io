Symptoms of Coronavirus

COVID-19 affects people in different ways. Majority of infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and get better without being hospitalized.
The incubation period for the virus ranges from 6-14 days. Recovery from covid symptoms takes about to weeks for people with mild cases and ranges form 3-6 week for people with serious cases.

As stated by the World Health Organisation (WHO), the following are critical symptoms of Covid-19:-

Dry Cough

Breathing difficulty

Headache

Loss of taste/smell.

Fever



Some minor symptoms are-

  • Excess Sneezing
  • Aches and Pains
  • Diarrhoea
  • Chest pains
